Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025 — This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

From Discord

When you add the Bot to a server or log in to the dashboard, we collect:

  • Discord user ID, username, and avatar (for authentication and display)
  • Discord server (guild) ID and name
  • Server member roles (to determine permissions)
  • Voice channel audio (only in servers where AI Voice Dispatch is configured and active — audio is transcribed in real time and not stored)

From Bot Usage

When members use bot commands, we store data necessary to provide the feature:

  • CAD characters, vehicles, and firearms linked to your Discord user ID
  • Strike records, verification status, and economy balances
  • Server configuration settings entered by administrators

From Purchases

When you purchase a Premium subscription or lifetime key, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your card number or billing details. We store:

  • Stripe customer ID and subscription/payment ID
  • Discord user ID of the purchaser
  • Guild ID the key is activated on
  • Purchase date and tier (monthly/lifetime)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate you on the dashboard
  • To process and verify Premium access
  • To enforce our Terms of Service

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Retention

Bot data (CAD records, strikes, economy balances, configuration) is retained as long as the bot is present in your server or until you request deletion. Discord user authentication tokens are stored only for the duration of your session.

To request deletion of your personal data, contact us via Discord support server.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process data on our behalf:

  • Discord — authentication and bot platform (discord.com/privacy)
  • MongoDB Atlas — database hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • OpenAI / Groq — voice transcription and AI dispatch responses (audio is sent for transcription and not retained by us)
  • Koyeb — server hosting

5. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no system is perfectly secure. Use of the Service is at your own risk.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
