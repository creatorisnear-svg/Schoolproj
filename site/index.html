Features

Everything your RP server needs

Built specifically for GTA 5 roleplay communities. Every feature works out of the box.

AI Voice Dispatch Premium
Officers speak in patrol voice channels. The bot transcribes, generates realistic dispatcher responses, and updates a live status board automatically.
CAD & 911 System
Full character and vehicle database. Civilians file 911 calls, officers respond and attach. Auto-cleanup and repeat announcements for unresponded calls.
Member Verification
Customizable intake form with PSN/Xbox tags, custom questions, staff approval workflow, and automatic role assignment on approval.
Economy System
Full currency ecosystem: cash and bank, work, crime, robberies, gambling, role-based income, shops, inventory, and 140+ GTA V vehicles.
Priority Tracker
Real-time priority event board with cooldown timers, staff controls, and a live-updating embed in your dedicated priority channel.
Strike System
Multi-level strikes with configurable punishments: timeouts, kicks, or bans. Automatic DMs and log entries for every action.
Ticket Support
Custom ticket types with role-based access, automatic private channel creation, and a clean close/archive workflow. Up to 5 types free.
Civilian Jobs
Job board with shift-based role assignments and automatic expiry. Members check in and out; roles are granted and removed automatically.
Applications Premium
Staff application panels with a full DM Q&A flow. Staff review submissions with Accept/Deny buttons. Roles assigned automatically on approval.
RP Calendar
Schedule and display weekly roleplay sessions with timezone conversion and a clean calendar view members can reference at any time.
Role Management
Role requests, reaction roles, anti-promoting, voice mover, and welcome system. Every community tool your RP server needs.
Web Dashboard
Full control panel to enable features, configure every module, and manage channels without touching Discord. All from your browser.
